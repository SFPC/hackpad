About a message containing the medium conditions

 

Explore how a pattern and a piece of software can encode environmental conditions.

A pattern containing redundant sub-patterns is sent together with a piece of software. The software runs computer vision to analyze the pattern, and will only display true when the environmental conditions (light sources, angle, reflections) are exactly the same as the ones the sender used in the moment of encrypting the conditions into the software.

Each sub pattern is a condition in series (x && y && z) with the others to confirm that the pattern is recognized. Only if all sub patterns are recognized, the master pattern is true. This will only happen when the receiver has recreated the exact same conditions that the sender used to capture the pattern.

Maybe this relates in some way to relativity? Prior to Einstein, no one could explain why when you accelerate towards a body, time speeds up and when you move away from it, time appears to slow down. This was because it was (incorrectly) assumed that time was a constant. Einstein was able to reconsider this assumption and suggest that actually time dilates - ie. it is not constant and rather the space/time relationship is the constant. So I’m not quite sure where this tangent is going...but I guess that sampling assumes a constant flow of time?
